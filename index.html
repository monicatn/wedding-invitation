Growing up as Vietnamese Americans, Cecillia and Paul opted to share their announcement in two languages.

These bilingual wedding invitations were crafted, heavily revised, and gifted to my older sister, Cecillia, as a wedding present. With a production time of two weeks, 240 3-page invitations were produced at a cost of $250. The invitations include return envelopes for RSVP and postage stamps.

Client: Paul & Cecillia Nguyen
Paper Stock: Arjowiggins Curious Particles Gold & Translucents Gold Iridescent
Date: Spring 2017
Category: Branding
